Originally known as Studio 1 Dance Fitness, Rush was founded in 2014 by Careen and Anna. They enlisted Tada to craft a visual brand identity and supporting graphic system to help catapult this small-but-expanding business into its next 5-year plan: to become the leading in-person dance-inspired group fitness class in Auckland, while also gaining a strong global online presence.
